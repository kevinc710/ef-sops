💸 Refund Policy (SOP 2)

Core rule non-negotiable

Refund only the discounted product subtotal — never the pre-discount price, never the total including shipping.

Example: Item $69.99 → Discount -$6.99 → Net paid $62.99. Refund = $62.99. Not $69.99. Not $67.99 (with shipping).

Steps to process a refund in Shopify

  1. Open the order → click Refund
  2. Select the correct quantity
  3. Leave shipping unchecked (do not refund shipping)
  4. Verify amount matches the discounted product subtotal
  5. Add internal note: "Refunded discounted product amount. Shipping not refunded per SOP."

Shipping refunds

🚨 Manager approval required — except when the wrong item was shipped or the order was confirmed lost due to company/carrier error.

💳 Store Credit (SOP 1)

Eligibility — ALL must be true

  • Order is confirmed Delivered in carrier tracking
  • Customer agrees in writing to accept store credit
  • No active chargeback or payment dispute
  • Order is not flagged for fraud

Credit amount = product subtotal only

Include: item subtotal after discounts. Exclude: shipping, taxes, insurance/protection fees, payment processing fees.

How to issue in Shopify

  1. Go to Products → Gift Cards
  2. Click "Issue gift card"
  3. Enter exact product subtotal (after discounts)
  4. Add internal note: "Store credit issued per customer agreement. Shipping not included."
  5. Email the code to the customer
🛡️ HRR Investigation Process

When to trigger an investigation

If a customer claims an item is missing, damaged, or empty package, do not refund immediately. Follow this process:

Required evidence from customer

  • Clear photos of the package exterior, including any damage
  • Photos of the shipping label
  • Photos of all sides of the packaging
  • Photos of all contents received

Closure rules

  • Do not auto-refund unless you literally cannot fulfill the order without the customer's response
  • If the customer placed an order and hasn't confirmed a requested change — ship what they originally ordered
  • Do not let urgency, threats, or pressure bypass the investigation process
  • If customer comes back after closure — reopen and handle normally
  • Mark ticket: "Closed - No Response"
Template — Damaged/Missing Shipment Investigation
Hi [Customer Name], Thank you for your follow-up. We understand your concern and take claims involving damaged, missing, or incomplete shipments seriously. However, we are unable to issue a refund solely based on an email claim without first completing our standard investigation process. At this time, we are reviewing the shipment details with our fulfillment and carrier records. To continue the investigation, please provide: • Clear photos of the package exterior, including any damage • Photos of the shipping label • Photos of all sides of the packaging • Photos of all contents received Once we receive this information, we will review the claim promptly and determine the appropriate resolution based on the findings. We appreciate your cooperation and patience while this process is completed. Best regards, Ellison & Fitz Support
